Ephys Broken Down

Electrophysiology

Electrophysiology is one of the most powerful set of tools in modern neuroscience, but to many ephys data are challenging to interpret and analyze. Personally, I didn't become fluent in the language of ephys after learning to patch. It is my goal to make the interpretation of this sort of data more accessible to neuroscienists who are trained primarily in molecular biology and behavior techniques. coming soon....
